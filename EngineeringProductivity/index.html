Engineering Productivity

Productivity improvement of many of our major industries has become a national priority. Much of the responsibility for improving the productivity of resources within organizations will fall on the scrum masters and managers. Central to this challenge is the development and implementation of measurement and improvement strategies for the engineers themselves.
